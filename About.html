About us


Helloo World is a youth run, intersectional platform where
activism meets code. Learn more about us here!





Mission

We encourage visitors to...

Interact with our site’s activities about important topics,

Learn more about activism and coding, and invite them to

Create things through code that address (or even solve!) today’s big social, political, and economic problems.




A message

Every idea starts somewhere. When you first start coding, it usually starts with the words "Hello World."

I often look for opportunities to code up a cool project. When I’m not doing that, I’m catching up on current events and topics that push for good change. I’ve always wanted to put coding and activism together, but I never knew how to. After a handful of ideas that never reached their starts and a few projects at a coding camp, Helloo World began to develop.

I found that I loved making and tinkering with interactive activities. I enjoyed learning more about topics and creating visualized content rather than displaying it through plain text. I enjoyed finding new ways to solve problems and build things with code. I wanted to share that with the world.

We hope you’re ready to say “Helloo World” as you explore the world through our platform! Me and my team hope that Helloo World delivers fun, teaches more about code and the world around us, and inspires you to make your own creations that push towards making the world a better place.

Gabby Banaag
Helloo World founder
